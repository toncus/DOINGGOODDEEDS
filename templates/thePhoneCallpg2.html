#THEGOODLIFE = GOOD PEOPLE DOING GOOD DEEDS/THINGS FOR THE GOOD OF ALL

The Phone Call

He said she called from the future. She talked of love and kindness and how it has lead to the most important scientific breakthrough of all time.

She spoke of how people of the 21st century are inexplicably linked to this breakthrough and how important it is to love one another.

She described how most people in the future had hardened their hearts and grown very intolerant and hateful.

Attendance at worldly religions had fallen to all time lows.

Governments around the world noticed the problem and asked scientists of the world to help.

Scientists took up the challenge of investigating things like love, peace and joy.

They especially looked into why some things like love, knowledge and grattitude grew when shared.

A little known scientist, Professor Haverex, momentarily tapped into a time link with the happiest people of the 21st century.

While not knowing all the details, the discovery seemed to tap into the infinite power of love.

When the discovery broke: Things like war, hate and disease slowly vanished from the planet.
