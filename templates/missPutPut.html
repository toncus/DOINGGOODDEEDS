THANK YOU MISS PUT-PUT!!! ( Said with affection )

It was Decemeber 2017. In a season where everywhere we see the colors Red and Green, I am ashamed to admit, I was only seeing RED!

It was one of those days where nothing went right.
To top off matters, just when it seemed things couldn't get any worse, I encountered Miss Put Put.
She was an old good-natured lady, with a warm smile, and the speed of molasses, on line at the Farmers Market ahead of me.

"Why? Why was this lady in front of me? Can she move any faster?" were my thoughts.
Finally she empties her basket onto the conveyor belt. What does she do next?

The stack of baskets below didn't have any room. She takes them out, moving very slowly and brings them to the front bagging area!!!!......IT SEEMED LIKE FOREVER!!!

When she was done and it was my turn at the cashier, the cashier was lightning fast...VERY NICE!!

That was until, she flung my radishes and they went off the edge!!!!!

As the ANGER BUILT INSIDE ME, I proceeded to the end to retrieve my radishes from the floor.
I was all prepared to give this cashier A PIECE OF MY MIND.

WHAT DO I DISCOVER? The baskets Miss Put-Put moved, CAUGHT MY RADISHES !!!!

How could this be? How could she have known? She was just doing a good deed right?

Moral: Don't be so quick to judge others...especially if you are having a bad day.

Thank you Miss Put-Put whoever you are!
