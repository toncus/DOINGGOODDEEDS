A wave of love came over all the people. People with illnesses for years were being healed and even the most bitter people started loving.

The man asked, "That sounds great, so why are you calling?"

She explained how things had to happen for the future magic to occur.

He will go out and speak with many and to his surprise, others had already written about this very call.

So we ask, "Could the great discovery of the future be because of something you did or will do?
