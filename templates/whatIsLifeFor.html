What Is This Life For?

Just about everyone has a theory of what this life is for. Some will state quite frankly, "It's about getting more!"

They tell you,"The one who passes with all the toys, will surely win the prize".
One wonders what prize that might be when the body dies.

Are we suddenly whisked away for a job well done? Or do those toys simply pass on to those who just begun?

Maybe it is about "giving more" to grow our inner light.
Maybe kindness, love and joy make our souls shine bright.

Could there be a moral here, that by giving,
we receive more than we ever did before?

Are kindness, love and joy the keys...to the universe's magic door?"

Could things like kindness, love, and joy stay with us all the way?
Could they even stay with us when we pass one day?

Maybe this inner light of kindness, love and joys, is the master prize to accumulate, instead of this life's toys!
