The Magic Mirror of Kindness

Look into a mirror and your physical reflection will show,
and though it shows alot, it's lacking, don't you know?

It shows the scar you had the day you scraped your knee,
but says nothing of that helpless bird that you let go free.

It shows unflattering wrinkles of age and advancement of your years,
but says nothing of the countless deeds you've done, and many happy tears.

Let's now look into the magic mirror, which shows this info too, the beautifully good deeds you did, and the beauty that is you.

It shows the lessons you learned when you were still a little tot, the lessons that made you who you are, and the ones that you forgot.

The masterpiece within you is the story this mirror tells.
The beautiful angel within you, is the story this mirror yells.
