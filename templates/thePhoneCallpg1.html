The Phone Call

This story you are about to experience is something we believe to be factual, yet it hasn't actually happened yet.

It is a story we feel we have known all our lives and yet we haven't read or heard of it before.

It is a story about an obscure phone call received by a homeless man, thought to be mute, on a phone, thought to be out of order.

What was communinicated is nothing short of amazing.

The man receiving the call, broke down expressing such grattitude for life, he could not stop telling everyone he met.
He was also suddenly cured and no longer mute.

What could have caused such an event? Was it a hoax?

We hardly think so, because even as we tell this story from behind our keyboard, it feels like some unknown force is spoon-feeding this story to us.

The man receiving the call that day, described a woman with the most beautiful voice.

He described her words as magical because each word filled him with intense love, grattitude and peace.
