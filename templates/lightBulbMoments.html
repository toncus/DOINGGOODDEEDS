Light Bulb Moments

Many a teacher will attest to that moment a child gleams with understanding light.

When after many attempts to think on something, their inner light shined bright.

When out of the darkness of not knowning, they came to understand.

It was if a light switch was turned on inside them by a tender loving hand.

"Epiphany" they call this magic, to which the child has aspired.

They know somehow deep inside that their magic was inspired.

Working hard had played its part and goodness was a key.

Then a missing puzzle piece of life was granted on to thee.

Preparing the child for epiphanies, that will come in future days,
is the teacher of today, showing them kindness loving ways.

To all teachers that inspire these moments of epiphany loving magic,
your efforts are truly of greatness, you are awesome and fantastic.
